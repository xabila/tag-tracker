Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 23, 2025

Your privacy is important to us. At TagTrack, we have a fundamental principle: we do not collect or store any of your personal data. All data generated by the app is stored locally on your device and never leaves it.

Data Storage

All your habit data, settings, and progress are stored exclusively on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. We do not have access to this information. If you delete the app, this data will be permanently removed.

Third-Party Services

We use RevenueCat for managing premium subscriptions. RevenueCat may collect anonymous, non-personally-identifiable information for the purpose of processing payments and ensuring subscription access across your devices. Please refer to the RevenueCat Privacy Policy for more details.

Permissions

TagTrack requires access to the NFC reader on your device to function. This permission is used solely for scanning NFC tags to log your habits. The app does not require access to your microphone, location, contacts, or any other personal information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
