What is a Phygital Habit Tracker? Qu'est-ce qu'un habit tracker phygital ? ¿Qué es un habit tracker phygital?

February 7, 2026 · 8 min read 7 février 2026 · 8 min de lecture 7 de febrero de 2026 · 8 min de lectura

1. Why traditional habit trackers aren't enough

Most habit trackers are purely digital. You open an app, find the right habit in a list, tap a checkbox, and hope you remember to come back tomorrow.

This creates friction (too many steps) and makes it easy to forget, because nothing in your physical environment reminds you of the habit at the right time.

2. What "phygital" really means

"Phygital" combines physical and digital.

In many experiences (retail, events, museums), NFC tags and QR codes are used to connect real-world places or objects with digital content, actions, or data.

A phygital habit tracker applies the same principle to your routines.

3. How phygital habit tracking works

With a phygital habit tracker like TagTrack, you attach NFC tags or QR codes to real objects in your environment: your water bottle, coffee machine, nightstand, front door, or gym bag.

Each tag is linked to one or several habits in the app.

When you tap the tag with your iPhone, the app instantly logs the action, updates your streak, and shows your progress—without navigating menus or searching for the right habit.
