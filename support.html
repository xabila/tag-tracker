Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I set up an NFC tag?

On the main screen of the application, you will find an 'NFC' button. Tap this button and follow the instructions to associate a new NFC tag with a habit.

What kind of NFC tags are supported?

TagTrack works with most modern, writable NFC tags (like NTAG213, NTAG215, or NTAG216). We recommend using stickers for convenience.

How is my data stored?

All your data is stored locally and privately on your device. We do not have access to it. See our Privacy Policy for more information.

How do I cancel my subscription?

All subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. You can cancel your subscription at any time by going to your iPhone's Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions.
